About Me

Welcome To My Digital Space!

I am Waliur Rahman, a Graduate Research Assistant at the Autonomic Computing Lab, The University of Arizona, specializing in AI, cybersecurity, and network analysis. My research, funded by NSF CAC and NSF WISPER, focuses on AI-driven reputation management, SEO intelligence, and compliance verification in communication standards.

I also contributed to the InSuRE Program (NSA-funded), working on network security, covert communication in IPv6, and machine learning-based traffic analysis. Passionate about AI and cybersecurity, I strive to develop innovative, real-world solutions for the future.
